Islands Restaurant Ala Moana Center, Honolulu

Visit our Honolulu Islands Restaurant located in Ala Moana Center on the 4th floor Ho'okipa Terrace.

Our Specialties

Enjoy daily happy hour, on our huge lanai or in the bar, for specials on appetizers, tropical drinks, wine and cold draft beer. The lanai is also great for big parties and for watching sports on our big TVs. Islands gourmet burgers like the spicy Kilauea burger and fries pair perfectly with ice cold beer or our wide selection of tropical drinks and cocktails.
